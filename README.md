# ISBA 4796: Capstone Proposal Development

Loyola Marymount University · College of Business Administration · Information Systems &
Business Analytics · **Summer 2026, Session 2**

A 1-unit, fully asynchronous course that gets you to a vetted capstone proposal before ISBA
4797 begins. You set up a professional, AI-assisted development environment, gather
requirements from a real stakeholder, write a product requirements document, and run a short
Scrum sprint that ends in a live sprint review.

## How the course runs

- **Week 1:** onboard through the [AI Dev Workflow Tutorial](https://github.com/LMU-ISBA/ai-dev-workflow-tutorial/tree/main/v4) and a self-discovery AI interview.
- **Weeks 2–3:** learn Scrum and shape an individual problem, with one 30-minute 1:1 (Week 2 or 3). Teams form at the end of Week 3.
- **Weeks 4–6:** work as a team in a 45-minute meeting each week: discovery, writing the PRD, and a final sprint review.
